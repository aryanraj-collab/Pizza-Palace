The Legacy

Origins of Fire

In 1889, Naples gave birth to a legend. At Pizza Palace, we keep that flame alive. Our ovens are built using volcanic rock from Vesuvius, ensuring that every crust has that signature leopard-spot char that defines true Neapolitan pizza.

The Art of Cheese

We don't buy cheese; we curate it. Our Mozzarella di Bufala arrives weekly from Campania. The creamy texture and slight tang are the result of centuries of tradition, hand-pulled to perfection to melt seamlessly over our signature sauce.
